Exercise 320 - JS, Marc Fachini intro

This exercise is about being creative.

The purpose of this exercise is to practice creative coding.

I am currently working on a minimalistic project for Marc Fachini, a website presenting his new album, Himmelmekanik. I could use some help with the page intro.

The project consists of 5 scenes, which will each, in turn, fill out the entire browser window.

First an intro (which I would like you to help me out with). After that comes an audio player, which plays side A of the LP. If you move the mouse while listening, the music stops, possibly replaced by the sound of a pickup moving sideways. The point is, focus on the music – take your time. After that, a short text, while we turn the record. And then a second audio player with side B and finally a thanks for listening.

So, in the exercise page you find the text for the intro on the site. We need this text to apear in a slow and relaxed manner – possibly with a touch of "handmade" feel to it – and then a bunch of megacool on top. You can use the whole window and it's ok to have to scroll down in this first scene. The background must be white.

It ok to get inspiration from others, but try to visualize how it could be and then try build it. The process of transforming and idea into actual code is an interesting one.
